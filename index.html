About

Level Up is a creative tech conference that explores the process of creating and shipping modern software products. The conference will feature practical experience by industry experts, over two days, covering four themes; ideate, design, build, and launch. Join us to meet interesting people, make new friends, and learn new skills you can show off at home.

Tickets are extremely limited.

Topical Topics
Show & tell: personalized

A wide range of topics will be explored including everything from server side javascript, to cutting edge trends in design. Plenty of time will be allotted after each talk for questions and discussion.

An Intimate Experience
Guaranteed to meet new people

Top down we've designed Level Up to be an inspirational experience. The venue is walking distance from both the hotel and a vibrant downtown district where you can eat, shop, or grab a beer at any one of several dozen local pubs.

Power Packed
Prepare for a knowledge bomb

Talks will be short and focused; each between 30 & 55 minutes long. Each speaker will have full knowledge of those that present before them, and will build off the concepts conveyed by previous speakers.
