Level Up is a creative tech conference where you'll explore the latest trends in web design & development with a focus on topics that are practical and immediately valuable to your everyday work. You'll meet interesting people, make new friends, and learn new skills you can show off at home.

It's a unique event happening in famous resort town located only 3 hours from both New York and Boston. LevelUp will be an intimate affair and so tickets are extremely limited.

Register today to find out more!

Cross Train
Twice the speakers for your buck.

Modern web designers & developers must be interested and involved in a wide range of subject areas to excel in their craft. Moreover, that cross-pollination of expertise is one of the driving forces behind the rate of change in our craft.

Because of this, the topics at Level Up are purposefully varied in subject but share a the common theme that each both practical and immediately applicable to your everyday work. A wide range of topics will be explored including everything from server side javascript, container virtualization, and client side MVC frameworks alongside topics like how innovative products can use innovative design to drive interest and engagement.

Power Packed
Prepare for a knowledge bomb

The conference will happen over two days and follow a single track, mixed length format.

Topics will be short and focused; each between 20 and 40 minutes long. Each speaker will have full knowledge of those that present before them, and will attempt to build off the concepts conveyed by previous speakers. Plenty of time will be allotted after each talk for for questions and discussion.

Finally, local foodies will prepare lunch and snacks each day to to ensure everyone is properly satiated (and hydrated!)

An Intimate Experience
Guaranteed to meet new people

Top down we've designed Level Up to be an inspirational experience. The venue is located walking distance from both the hotel and a vibrant downtown district where you can eat, shop, or grab a beer at any one of three dozen local pubs.

Attendee only social events are scheduled each night to ensure that we'll get to know each other, have time to discuss the topics we've learned, and have a bit of fun.

Last, you'll enjoy the beautiful resort town of Saratoga during the peak of New York's Fall Season. (And if you enjoy the outdoors, you're only a 30 minutes from the biggest park in lower United States.)
