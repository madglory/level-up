Level Up

Creative Tech Conference 2014

In Association with MadGlory

Sign me the hell up

Held at Criterion Cinemas 11 & BTX in Saratoga Springs New York, Level Up is a creative tech conference that touches on the latest trends in web design & development, with a focus on topics that are immediately applicable to your day job. You’ll be learning new things rather than rehashing what’s already out there. Efficiency level over 9,000.

What to expect, when you’re expecting:

Two Heads > Ove
Twice the speakers for your buck.

Rather than having all the focus on you, why not grab a friend? The ability to entertain two speakers at the same time allows for an interesting talk. Designers with Developers, two developers, two designers, or be a solo superstar – we’re open to it all.

Designer Insight
Not a photoshop 101 class.

Anyone can find tutorials on how to add drop shadows to text (please don't). What we're preaching here is a little deeper than that – developers and designers alike stand to learn a thing or two about process and secret designer things.
